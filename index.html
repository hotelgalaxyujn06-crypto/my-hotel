Explore Nearby Destinations

Discover famous attractions and spiritual destinations located near Ujjain.

Indore

Explore Madhya Pradesh's largest city, famous for Rajwada Palace, Sarafa Night Market, and delicious street food. Approximately 55 km from Ujjain.

Omkareshwar Temple

Visit one of the twelve sacred Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, situated on the beautiful Mandhata Island. Around 140 km from Ujjain.

Maheshwar

Discover the majestic Maheshwar Fort, scenic Narmada River Ghats, and the world-famous Maheshwari handloom sarees. About 145 km from Ujjain.
